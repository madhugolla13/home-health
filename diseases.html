Remedies for Minor Diseases

Cold

Common cold can be relieved with warm fluids and spices.

  • Ginger Tea: Drink hot ginger tea 2-3 times a day.
  • Steam Inhalation: Inhale steam with a few drops of eucalyptus oil.
  • Garlic: Eat raw garlic or add it to soup to boost immunity.

Cough

Soothe your throat and reduce coughing naturally.

  • Honey and Lemon: Mix honey and lemon juice in warm water.
  • Turmeric Milk: Drink warm milk with a pinch of turmeric before bed.
  • Tulsi (Holy Basil): Chew tulsi leaves or make a tea.

Headache

Ease tension and relieve headaches safely.

  • Peppermint Oil: Massage a drop of peppermint oil on temples.
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water; dehydration causes headaches.
  • Ginger Tea: Reduces inflammation and pain.

Stomach Pain

Calm your digestive system with these remedies.

  • Cumin (Jeera) Water: Boil cumin seeds in water and drink.
  • Ajwain (Carom Seeds): Chew a pinch of ajwain with warm water.
  • Peppermint Tea: Helps relax stomach muscles.

Fever

Help bring down body temperature and stay hydrated.

  • Cold Compress: Apply a wet cloth on the forehead.
  • Hydration: Drink coconut water, broths, and juices.
  • Basil Tea: Boil basil leaves with ginger and drink warm.

Hair Fall

Strengthen hair roots and reduce hair loss naturally.

  • Fenugreek (Methi) Seeds: Soak seeds overnight, grind into a paste, apply on the scalp for 30 minutes, then wash off. Strengthens hair roots.

Mouth Ulcer

Soothe painful mouth sores with these natural remedies.

  • Honey: Apply raw honey directly on the ulcer to speed up healing.
  • Salt Water Rinse: Rinse your mouth with warm salt water to reduce inflammation.
  • Coconut Oil: Apply coconut oil on the affected area to soothe pain.

Gastric

Alleviate bloating, acidity, and gas with natural home remedies.

  • Fennel Seeds (Saunf): Chew half a teaspoon of roasted fennel seeds after meals to aid digestion and reduce gas.
  • Ginger Tea: Boil fresh ginger in water and drink it warm. Ginger naturally reduces inflammation and soothes the stomach.
  • Warm Water with Lemon: Drinking a glass of warm water with a squeeze of fresh lemon in the morning helps flush out toxins and neutralize stomach acid.

Iron Deficiency

Combat fatigue and boost your iron levels naturally with these remedies.

  • Iron-Rich Foods: Incorporate more spinach, lentils, beans, and fortified cereals into your diet.
  • Vitamin C Boost: Eat foods high in Vitamin C (like citrus fruits or tomatoes) alongside iron-rich foods to dramatically improve absorption.
  • Avoid Tea/Coffee with Meals: Tannins in tea and coffee can inhibit the absorption of iron, so drink them between meals instead.

Kidney Stones

Support kidney health and help manage stone symptoms naturally.

  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water throughout the day. Staying hydrated is the most crucial step in passing stones and preventing new ones.
  • Lemon Juice: Add fresh lemon juice to your water. Lemons contain citrate, which helps prevent calcium stones from forming.
  • Apple Cider Vinegar: Mix a couple of tablespoons in water. The citric acid can help dissolve kidney stones and alkalinize blood and urine.

Leg Pain

Relieve soreness and muscle aches in your legs with simple remedies.

  • Warm Compress & Epsom Salt: Soak your legs in a warm Epsom salt bath to relax muscles and reduce inflammation.
  • Oil Massage: Gently massage the legs with warm mustard, olive, or coconut oil to improve blood circulation and ease pain.
  • Elevation & Rest: Elevate your legs above heart level using pillows to reduce swelling and improve blood flow back to the heart.

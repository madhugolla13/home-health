Safety Precautions

While home remedies are natural and generally safe, it is crucial to follow basic guidelines to ensure your well-being.

  • Do Not Overdose

    Natural doesn't mean harmless in large quantities. Always use remedies in moderation as prescribed.

  • Consult a Doctor

    If symptoms persist for more than a few days, or if they worsen, seek professional medical advice immediately.

  • Check for Allergies

    Before trying a new remedy, ensure you are not allergic to any of the ingredients (e.g., nuts, certain herbs, pollen).

  • Maintain Hygiene

    Always wash your hands and ensure the utensils and ingredients you use are clean and fresh.

  • Caution with Children & Pregnant Women

    Many remedies suitable for adults are not safe for children or pregnant women. Always double-check safety before use.
